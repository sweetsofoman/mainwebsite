Our Story

Where Heritage Meets Modern Taste

Sweets of Oman UAE is a passionate confectionery distributor and producer deeply rooted in the rich sweet traditions of the Arabian Gulf. We bring together authentic recipes, premium ingredients, and modern craftsmanship.

From our own chocolate brands — Naqko and Zoho — to our beloved pistachio kunafa and curated confections, every product reflects our commitment to quality and cultural authenticity.
